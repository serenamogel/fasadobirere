 Posted on March 21, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  780 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Box Jellyfish is the world’s most venomous animal, and death can occur within minutes of a sting. There are 51 species of box jellyfish, and four of them; Chironex fleckeri, Carukia barnesi, Malo kingi, and Chironex yamaguchii – are extremely venomous. Since 1883, when records of box jellyfish-related deaths began, these box-shaped, gelatinous carnivores have claimed the lives of hundreds of people.
