Graham Patrick Martin Biography

Graham Patrick Martin is an American actor best known for his roles as Eldridge on Two and a Half Men from 2010 to 2012, and as former adolescent hustler Rusty Beck on The Closer’s series finale and spinoff series, Major Crimes, from 2012 to 2018. From 2007 through 2009, Martin starred as Bill Engvall’s eldest son in the sitcom The Bill Engvall Show. Martin appeared in the 2007 film Jack Ketchum’s The Girl Next Door and the 2010 film Rising Stars.

Martin attended New York City’s Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School. “I’m a New Yorker from New Orleans who lives in Los Angeles,” he said in a TNT Newsroom interview when asked to identify himself. Martin revealed that his older sister was the first in his family to become interested in acting when asked what inspired him to perform. Martin, his two brothers, and his sister attended French Woods, a performing arts summer camp where:

He came for the water sports and horseback riding, but while he was there, he ended up auditioning for my first play. He was eight years old at the time, and he was cast in The King and Him, my first musical. Until he was fifteen, he did three musicals every summer at French Woods.
