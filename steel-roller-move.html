Steel Roller (Japanese: アイアンローラー Iron Roller) is a damage-dealing Steel-type move introduced in Generation VIII. In Generation IX, it is the signature move of Iron Treads.

Effect

Steel Roller deals damage and removes any active terrain. If no terrain is active upon the move's execution, it will fail.

Generation VIII

Pokémon Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl

Steel Roller cannot be selected in a battle.
