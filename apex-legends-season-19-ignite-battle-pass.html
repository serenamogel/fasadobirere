Apex Legends Season 19 Ignite Battle Pass: First Look, Price &amp; Release Date

 Posted on May 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  341 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Season 19 is here with all new premium items, cosmetics, and a revamped Battle Pass.

Respawn has been going strong with the constant updates to Apex Legends. The popular Battle Royale game is entering its 19th season with all new goodies for the player base.

Their ‘freemium’ model pays dividends as the cosmetic items in Apex Legends are more popular than ever. This time, Season 19’s Battle Pass includes new cosmetic items, sky emotes, banners, seasonal trackers, music packs, and more to keep things fresh. Here’s everything you need to know about Ignite.

Season 19 Ignite Battle Pass First Look

The theme for this event is the release of conduit. The all-new electrifying legend seems to have influenced cosmetics with a similar godly aura. A touch of royalty and divinity can be found in every cosmetic for the Premium Battle Pass. Take a look for yourself!

Rampart Skin and Cosmetics

Wattson Skin and Cosmetics

Reactive Skin: Rampage

Respawn will give players the first Reactive skin after reaching level 25. However, to unlock the second Reactive Rampage skin, players must reach 110 levels like before.

Skydive Emote

Free Rewards

Even without buying the Battle Pass, players can still unlock many rewards as they level up. At any point, the Pass can be purchased and all Premium Rewards up to that level will be automatically redeemed.

You can unlock a Mirage Character Skin, 7 Apex Packs, 11 Weapon Skins, 4 Load Screens, Win Trackers for all Legends, 2 Music Packs, 200 Apex Coins, Season Badge

Season 19 Ignite Battle Pass Price

Expect to pay around 950 Apex coins to unlock the season 19 Battle Pass, which equals approximately $10 based on previous Battle Passes.

Season 19 Ignite Battle Pass Release Date

The Battle Pass will be released alongside Season 19 on October 31, 2023.
