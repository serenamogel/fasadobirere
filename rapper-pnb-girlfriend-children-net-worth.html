PnB Rock was in a relationship with his girlfriend, Stephanie Sibounheuang.

Stephanie Sibounheuang is a well-known model, fashion designer, and entrepreneur. She was born on July 20, 1991, and is 31.

Stephanie and PnB Rock were arrested back in 2018 on drug charges. The pair was taken to the Bucks County Correctional Facility and had to pay $500,000 bail.

PnB Rock Net Worth And Career Details

The estimated net worth of PnB Rock is $3 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

PnB Rock was fond of cars and had a collection of luxury cars such as Ferrari 458 Italia, BMW 7 Series, Rolls Royce Dawn, Lamborghini Huracan, and Bentley Mulsanne.

The rapper was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, in December 1991.

About his rap career, he made his debut with the mixtape Real N*gga Bangaz in June 2014, signed a record deal with Atlantic Records in 2015, and released another mixtape, RnB3.

In 2016, Rolling Stone named PnB Rock on the list of 10 New Artists You Need to Know.

His next release in June 2016 was a single titled Selfish. This single reached #16 on the US Rap chart and #21 on the US R&B/Hip-Hop chart. It was certified platinum. 

His fourth mixtape, GTTM: Goin Thru the Motions, was released in January 2017, followed by his first album, TrapStar Turnt PopStar, in May 2019. It was featured in Ed Sheeran’s song, Cross Me.

PnB Rock’s single Horses (with Kodak Black and A Boogie wit da Hoodie) was certified platinum, and his single Gang Up (with Young Thug, 2 Chainz, and Wiz Khalifa) was certified gold.

Recently, Rock released a mixtape, SoundCloud Daze, in February 2022.
