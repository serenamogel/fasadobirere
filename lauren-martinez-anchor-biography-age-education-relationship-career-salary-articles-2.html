lauren martinez Biography

Lauren Martinez is an American anchor at the 5, 10 & 11p.m weeknight newscasts. During her three year tenure in Chico, she has anchored countless hours of wall-to-wall coverage of major catastrophic events, starting with the Oroville Dam Spillway Crisis in 2017. Then, in July of 2018, the Carr Fire spawned a ‘firenado’ that ripped into the city of Redding. It was followed three months later by the most recent disaster, The Camp Fire. All three experiences have made Lauren a stronger and a more humble journalist.

PARADISE IRRIGATION DISTRICT LOOKS FOR WATER-SAMPLE VOLUNTEERS

PARADISE, Calif. – The Paradise Irrigation District said it is planning intensified water sampling for the first two weeks in June.
