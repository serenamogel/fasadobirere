Who is Isabella Pollok?

Isabella Pollok has been described as Lawrence ‘Larry’ Ray’s top lieutenant. Isabella is 31 years old former Sarah Lawrence College student. Isabella is the daughter of Cynthia Pollok.

She is scheduled to be sentenced on 22 February 2023, at 11 am. She was designated by federal prosecutors in 2021 for reportedly conspiring with convicted sex-cult leader Ray to prey on others at Sarah Lawrence College, a small liberal arts school in Westchester County.

According to charging documents, Isabella Pollok was accused of helping Larry Ray lead an “enterprise, which targeted a group of college students for criminal exploitation and indoctrination.”

Pollok was 19 at the time and met the 50-year-old father of her housemate, Talia Ray. Talia’s father, Lawrence V. Ray had only just been released from prison in New Jersey.

Over the next 10 years of her life, both at Sarah Lawrence and after she graduated, Pollok and Ray ran an alleged sex trafficking and money laundering ring with cult-like aspects.

Isabella was among a group of Talia’s roommates and friends who fell under the influence of Larry.

By the time, she emerged as, Larry’s “trusted lieutenant” helping him psychologically and sexually manipulate her friends, first as he gained their trust and then as he utilized them.
