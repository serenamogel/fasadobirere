Introduction to Twenty One Pilots

Twenty One Pilots, often stylized as twenty øne piløts or 21 Pilots, is an American musical duo originating from Columbus, Ohio. The current lineup consists of Tyler Joseph and drummer Josh Dun. They gained fame with their unique blend of music that includes elements of indie pop, rock, and hip-hop.
