Mithun Chakraborty Biography, Early Life, Career

Chakraborty made his acting debut in Mrigayaa, an art-house play for which he received his first National Film Award for Best Actor in 1976. Chakraborty featured as Jimmy in the 1982 film Disco Dancer, which was a box office hit in India and the Soviet Union. Chakraborty is also known for his parts in films such as Suraksha, Sahhas, Wardat, Wanted, Boxer, Pyar Jhukta Nahin, Pyari Behna, Avinash, Dance Dance, Prem Pratigyaa, Mujrim, Agneepath, Yugandhar, The Don, and Jallaad, in addition to Disco Dancer. In the film Agneepath, he received the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actor for his portrayal of Krishnan Iyer Nariyal Paniwala.
