We were shocked to read this morning of the untimely death of The Bad Ideas’ Britt Adair, a famous musician.

Our thoughts are with her family and everyone who knew her from the KC music community. She is a supportive neighbor, a happy musician, a good listener, and a kind giver. Love to her entire family and social circle. There will soon be more details released about Britt Adair’s passing.

After a battle with illness, Britt Adair of Kansas City, Missouri, passed away Friday night. Britt Adair was a fantastic vocalist and singer for the well-known band “The Bad Ideas.”

Her cause of death was reportedly not immediately confirmed by the family at this time, but she was allegedly pronounced dead after complications from a thyroid condition.
