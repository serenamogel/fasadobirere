S. Al Dawsari Bio, Age, Career, Parents, Net Worth & More – Salem Al-Dawsari is a Saudi Arabian footballer who plays as a winger for Al-Hilal as well as for the Saudi national team.

S. Al Dawsari biography

When it comes to football in Saudi Arabia, you can’t ignore S. Al Dawsari. He is currently the captain of the Saudi Arabian senior national soccer team. Aside from making a name for himself in football, he has also played for some of the top clubs in Saudi Arabia and has also won quite some trophies and bagged some individual awards.

S. Al Dawsari career

Al-Dawsari began his career with Al-Hilal as a youth player. He joined Villarreal on loan in 2018 as part of a deal between the Saudi Arabian Football Federation and La Liga.

He only made one appearance in Spain, coming on as a substitute against Real Madrid in a 2-2 draw.

On November 24, Al-Dawsari scored the opening goal in an eventual 2-0 away win over Urawa Red Diamonds, which saw Al-Hilal win the title following a 3-0 aggregate victory; the title allowed them to qualify for the 2019 FIFA Club World Cup.

Al-Dawsari was called up to the Saudi Arabian national team for the 2014 FIFA World Cup qualifiers and scored his first international goal against Australia in an away match in 2012.

He was named to Saudi Arabia’s preliminary squad for the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia in May 2018.

On June 25, Al-Dawsari scored a late winning goal as his team defeated Egypt 2-1 in their final World Cup group stage match.

Al-Dawsari scored his first world cup goal against Argentina in 2022, making a jinking run around the box before finishing deftly past Emi Martinez to put Saudi Arabia ahead 2-1.
