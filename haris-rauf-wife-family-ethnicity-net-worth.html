Haris Rauf wife is a topic of interest for his fans and followers worldwide. Everyone is eager to know if he is committed or single.

Besides, he made his Twenty20 debut for the Lahore Qalandars in October 2018 at the 2018 Abu Dhabi T20 Trophy. A month later, the team selected him in the players’ draft for the 2019 Pakistan Super League tournament.

Haris has played in 15 ODIs, 50 T20 Internationals, four first-class, and 15 List-A cricket games.

He is one of the standout players in Pakistan’s World Cup 2022 finals against England at Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG), Australia.

Haris Rauf Wife: Is Pakistani Cricketer Married?

It is also likely that he is secretly dating someone and does not feel like sharing it with the public. Either way, he is officially a bachelor.

Learn About Haris Rauf Family And Ethnicity

Haris Rauf was born into a simple middle-class with no sports background. Although he was born in the Rawalpindi city of Punjab Province, his family is originally from Mansehra in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan.

Haris belongs to a Muslim family, and his Father’s name is Rauf Ahmed. The cricketer is a Pakistani national of mixed ethnicity, states Sports Brief.

What Is Haris Rauf Net Worth In 2023?

According to Sports Brief, Haris Rauf has a net worth of $3.69 million. However, the figure is yet to be confirmed by the cricketer.

Most of his wealth comes from his career as a professional cricketer. The 5 feet 11 inches tall athlete has been with the national side since 2020.

However, Haris began domestic cricket earlier, debuting with Lahore Qalandars in 2019. Since then, he has played for the Northern cricket team, Melbourne Stars, and Yorkshire County Cricket Club.

Haris is nicknamed 150 for his bowling ability to hit the said speed. In a press release issued by the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB), he said, “Nobody calls me Haris in the Northern team, everyone refers to me as 150, and I even write 150 in my autographs.”
